Ross, do you ever think about who’s really behind every transaction?
No matter how big the order or how slick the tech, commerce always comes down to people.
 
A single lipstick or a thousand-bag wholesale deal—it’s all driven by individuals, each in a different frame of mind. Whether they’re buying for themselves, their boss, a client, or a stranger, human emotion drives every decision.
 
From the maker to the marketer, the picker with a limp to the Gen Alpha kid unboxing your product on YouTube—it’s people all the way down.
 
That’s why understanding who you’re selling to—and why—is just as important as what you’re selling.
 
I’ve written a short piece on how to launch your D2C store with this in mind. It covers how to drive awareness, spark conversions, and turn your wholesale customers into allies instead of obstacles.
